Not Carbonara

It’s not carbonara! It’s much better...

One serving is generous

Ingredients

  • g spaghetti with 10 minutes cooking time
  • g grated Grana or Parmesan
  • egg
  • g smoked pancetta cubes
  • shallot
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • tablespoon olive oil
Servings: 1

Procedure

  • Bring a pot of salted water to a boil
  • Finely chop the shallot
  • Beat the whole egg with the Grana, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
  • Cook the spaghetti whole in the boiling water for 10 minutes
  • Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the shallot
  • Add the pancetta and let it brown over low heat
  • Add the drained spaghetti and toss with the shallot and pancetta
  • Turn off the heat, let cool slightly, and add the beaten egg
