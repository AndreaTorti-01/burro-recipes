Rice, Turkey, and Broccoli Cream

A healthy, creamy, and protein-rich dish.

One serving is filling

Ingredients

  • g broccoli
  • g turkey breast
  • g basmati rice
  • garlic clove
  • tablespoon grated Grana Padano
  • salt as needed
  • pepper as needed
  • paprika as needed
  • butter as needed
  • milk as needed
  • extra virgin olive oil as needed
Servings: 1

Procedure

  • Cut the broccoli and boil for 5 minutes in salted water
  • Remove the broccoli once cooked and cook the rice in the same water for 10 minutes
  • Blend the broccoli with oil, salt, pepper, paprika, grated Grana Padano, and garlic, adding milk to adjust the consistency
  • Cut the turkey breast into 1.5 cm thick slices and peel the garlic
  • Heat a steel pan or grill to high temperature, grease with oil and butter as desired
  • Cook the turkey slices for a few minutes on each side
  • Cut the turkey into strips and serve
