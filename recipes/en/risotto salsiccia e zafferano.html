Sausage and Saffron Risotto

A timeless classic

One serving is filling

Ingredients

  • g Carnaroli rice
  • handful grated Grana or Parmesan
  • handful diced shallot
  • g sausage
  • packet saffron
  • tablespoon olive oil
  • knob of butter
  • ml mixed meat broth
  • pinch of pepper
  • cheap white wine as needed
  • salt as needed
Servings: 1

Procedure

  • Remove the sausage casing
  • Bring the broth to a boil in a pot
  • Heat the oil in a large, deep pot
  • Sauté the shallot
  • Sauté the sausage over low heat, breaking it up with a wooden spoon
  • Add the rice and toast it over high heat, stirring constantly
  • Once toasted, deglaze with plenty of white wine
  • When the alcohol has evaporated, add broth to just cover the rice and cook over medium heat
  • Continuously add broth, keeping the level just above the rice, until fully cooked. If the broth runs low, dilute it with boiling water. Start tasting after 10 minutes!
  • When cooked, lower the heat, add the butter, saffron, and grated cheese, and stir until fully creamy. Taste! Add salt if needed, little by little.
