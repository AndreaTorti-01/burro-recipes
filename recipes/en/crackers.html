Crackers

A simple and quick recipe for spiced and nutritious crackers

One serving: about 20 crackers

Ingredients

  • g oats
  • g flour
  • g bran
  • level tablespoon rosemary
  • teaspoon oregano
  • teaspoon thyme
  • level teaspoon turmeric
  • level teaspoon paprika
  • heaping teaspoon salt
  • ml extra virgin olive oil
  • ml lukewarm water
Servings: 1

Procedure

  • Blend all the ingredients, except oil and water, in a blender or food processor. Do not overblend, or the final texture will be floury.
  • Gradually add oil and water and knead until achieving a uniform consistency.
  • Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (static).
  • Shape into uniform disks 0.5 cm thick and 5 cm in diameter, and place them close together on parchment paper.
  • When the oven is hot, bake at mid-height and monitor closely. Remove when the edges of the crackers begin to change color.
